# Product Vision

## What Openboard is

Openboard is the internal operating dashboard for Biketime projects.

Its job is to make ongoing work visible and manageable in one place:
- tasks
- todos
- development history
- project context

## Why it exists

We need a lightweight internal system that is:
- faster than a heavy PM tool
- easy to evolve with our own workflow
- tightly connected to how Adam and the AI team actually work

## Users

### Primary
- Adam — founder, operator, product owner

### Secondary
- TARS — planning, orchestration, documentation
- Erik — implementation
- Brock — QA and review

## Product principles

1. **Fast to use** — low friction, quick switching, quick capture
2. **Operational first** — support real daily work, not just pretty planning
3. **Simple over clever** — ship practical features first
4. **Built around our workflow** — Openboard should fit the team, not the other way around
5. **Source of clarity** — current work and decisions should be easy to find

## Core jobs to be done

- Capture ideas before they get lost
- Turn todos into structured tasks
- Track work across projects
- Keep a visible history of progress
- Support planning and review conversations
